About Us

Okure Piggery Farm is a professionally managed pig farming enterprise based in Awee Iyec A, Ngai Sub-county, Oyam District. The farm is committed to producing healthy, high-quality pigs through modern, sustainable, and ethical farming practices. We focus on proper breeding, balanced nutrition, disease prevention, and animal welfare to meet both commercial and household market demands.

About the Founder

Okure Piggery Farm was founded by Ojom Jonathan, an agribusiness entrepreneur with a strong interest in livestock farming and rural economic development. The farm was established with the vision of improving household incomes, promoting modern pig farming techniques, and supplying reliable, quality pigs to the local and regional market.

Through hands-on management, continuous learning, and a commitment to quality, the founder ensures that Okure Piggery Farm operates with professionalism, transparency, and long-term sustainability.

Vision & Mission

Our Vision

To be a leading provider of high-quality, healthy pork products while promoting sustainable and innovative pig farming practices in the region.

Our Mission

  • To raise pigs in a healthy, humane, and sustainable environment.
  • To provide customers with fresh, safe, and nutritious pork products.
  • To empower the local community through employment and training in modern pig farming techniques.
  • To continuously improve farm efficiency and productivity through innovation and best practices.

Our Services

  • Sale of piglets, growers, and mature pigs
  • Breeding services
  • Training and consultation on pig farming
  • Supply of quality pork on request
